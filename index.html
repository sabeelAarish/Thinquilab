What drives us!

India and Bharat, almost distinct countries, economies, processes. While the Indians enjoy the fruits of the liberalised economy the Bharat-wasis are marginally benefited owing to the lack of English, technology and formalised processes.

While the Indian startup ecosystem is aflush with teams/organisations looking for product-market fit, the Bharat-wasis are focussed on markets over products. They don't have a business plan on paper but are extremely business minded. MBA or not, but masters of their businesses. They get stuff done, everyday! Thinquilab is an accelerator for these Bharat-wasis. We support their rugged acumen with state-of-the-art strategy, processes, communications, systems, and technology.

We aim to ensure that the Bharat-wasis have a chance at leveraging the learnings and innovations of the Indians.
